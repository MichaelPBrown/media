MY LIFE


Football
















This is what got me going. Football was everything for me.
I started watching when I was young, I think I was around 7 years old when I really started loving the game.

My team that I have always followed is the Seattle Seahawks. I love everything about them, from their players to their jerseys.
I started playing almost immediately, playing here and there with friends.

Then I got to middle school. I found out there was a team, and I joined in an instant. I was out on the field for tryouts as soon as they were held.

I made it on the team, and ended up being a starter for our games at the wide reciever and cornerback positions.
I was enjoying every second of it for three years straight.

Then I came to D.tech where they don't have football, however, I am still going strong practicing every day just to walk on at college, and see where I can go from there.
